Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

This page covers the privacy practices of the-duke.org, the personal portfolio site of Matt Duke. The site is a static GitHub Pages page; what you see is what runs.

Short version: the site collects nothing about you. No accounts, no cookies, no analytics, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting. The page loads in your browser and that is the entire interaction.

What this site does

It displays three small apps Matt has built, with descriptions, hours-saved estimates, and links to each app's source repository on GitHub. Every link is user-initiated.

What this site does not do

  • It does not set cookies of any kind.
  • It does not run any analytics — no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Fathom, no self-hosted metrics.
  • It does not load tracking pixels, ad networks, or A/B testing tools.
  • It does not collect or transmit your IP address, location, device, or browser to any server controlled by Matt.
  • It does not have any form, login, or contact submission — nothing to type into.

What third parties may see

Two outside resources are referenced by the page and are subject to their privacy policies, not this one:

  • GitHub Pages hosts the static files. GitHub records standard web-server access logs (IP address, user agent, requested URL) per their privacy statement. Matt does not see those logs.
  • Google Fonts serves the Inter typeface from fonts.googleapis.com. Google's own font-server policy applies; see their Google Fonts FAQ on privacy. If you would prefer the page not contact Google, a system font fallback is in place — blocking fonts.googleapis.com in your browser will not break the site.

Linking out to the apps

The apps showcased here have their own privacy practices. Each links to the relevant source repository on GitHub. Find Me People, the browser extension, also has its own privacy policy stored in its repository.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them — consistent with the rest of this page, the site does not collect information from anyone.

Your rights under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws

Most data-protection laws give you rights to access, correct, and delete personal information held about you. Because this site holds no personal information about you, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If a regulator or auditor needs a written attestation to that effect, email matthewduke0@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes — for example if Matt ever adds privacy-respecting analytics or a contact form — the "Last updated" date above will change and the previous version of this page will remain available in the site's Git history.
